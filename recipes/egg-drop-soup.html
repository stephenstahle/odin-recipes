Egg Drop Soup


Egg drop soup for a cold day!

Ingredients

  • Chicken broth
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ginger
  • Garlic Powder
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Bring chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add the salt, pepper, ginger and garlic powder to the broth.
  3. Let simmer for about 2-3 minutes.
  4. When done, turn off the heat and mix in the beaten eggs! Enjoy!
