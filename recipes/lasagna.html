Lasagna


This is an amazing lasagna recipe that I came up with myself

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ricota Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. While that comes to a boil, go ahead and start browning your ground beef in a large skillet.
  3. Add your salt, pepper and italian seasoning to the ground beef and cook until done.
  4. Once the water has come to a boil, cook your lasagna noodeles per directions on the box.
  5. In a large caserole dish, you will start layering your ingredients.
  6. Start with a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of the dish to prevent burning.
  7. Start by layering your cooked noodles, top it with ricotta cheese, mozerall cheese and ground beef.
  8. Sprinkle cheese on top of that and start a new layer of noodles once that has been done.
  9. Repeat steps 6-8 until the caserole dish is full!
  10. Bake in an oven at 375 F for 1 hour.
