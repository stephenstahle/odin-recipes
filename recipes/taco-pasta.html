Taco Pasta


Treat yourself with this amazing mexican inspired pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Fire roasted tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Beef Broth
  • Cream Cheese
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Corn
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. (Once boiling, add your pasta and cook to al dente.
  2. While the water boils, brown your ground beef, onion and garlic in a large saucepan.
  3. Once brown, add the can of fire roasted tomatoes with your can of beef broth.
  4. Bring to a simmer and add a half stick of cream cheese.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Drain your can of corn and add into the saucepan
  7. Drain the pasta and add in the mixture in the saucepan.
  8. Servce and top with cilantro!
